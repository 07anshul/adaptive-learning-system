SECTION 5 — Notes / definitions

  • Mastery: mastery_score ≥ {{ metrics.time_to_mastery.definition.mastery_score_min }} and fragility_score ≤ {{ metrics.time_to_mastery.definition.fragility_score_max }}.
  • Fragile success: correctness=true plus slow/hints/low-confidence/high-fragility signal.
  • Recommendation effectiveness (approx): recomputed during replay (recommendations are not stored per attempt); “improved” means at least one of correctness/time/hints/mastery/fragility improved within the next 1–2 attempts in the same topic.
These are internal demo validation metrics, not rigorous research metrics.
